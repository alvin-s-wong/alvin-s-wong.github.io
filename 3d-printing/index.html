3D Printing

Big 3D printing fan here – started at the beginning of 2024.

What started out as a way to make custom tools and parts for woodworking – turned into a separate hobby and periodic obsession!

What I Print

With my FDM printer – I 3D print custom parts and tools for fixing, improving or inventing new tools and parts for woodworking, filmmaking, photography and randomly around the house. I'm a bit upgrade-obsessed.

I try to post my designs for others to openly use as well on STL sharing sites like thingiverse, thangs, and printables.

With my Resin printer (sometimes with FDM too) – I print miniatures and props and enjoy painting and making 3D artifacts for friends, family and myself.
