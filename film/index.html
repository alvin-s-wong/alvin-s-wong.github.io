Film

I produce videos for commercial and friends and family - mostly as a hobby out of pure passion. I've done a few paid projects, but it all started and still continues today with YouTube videos to talk about music production, software development and more.

I originally wanted to be a film and media composer, and instead of only being a part of the overall filmmaking process, I soon fell in love with the rest of the production – including cinematography and storytelling.
